About Us

With the explosion of media options, technology, interactivity & activation have become the buzzwords of communication. Clients look for communication solutions & also the experience of interacting with their target audience across multiple media. This is where J Square Promotions defines itself.

Founded and registered in 2009, The Company has emerged as one of the most reputed Tanzanian events and marketing companies. We create brand events, with a touch of magic, to inspire and transform your audience. We also deliver focused and turnkey solutions, from brand strategy and content development to creative, design, video, production, and program management.

Strategic & Creative Tool Guide

Strategic & creative tool is an internal process to assist us in being smart about the business and the brand at hand based on sixth senses. By being smart, we can define the business opportunity better, from which we can develop and execute ideas that activate demand for our clients.

It allows us to build meaningful experiences into the consumer’s life that not only connects with them personally and emotively, but also drives greater propensity to purchase as a result of the engagement they have with the Brand Experience.

What We Do

J Square Promotion Limited, a private agency, deals with experimental marketing activities – specially road shows, field activations, event management, field sales force management, point-of-sales activations, channel management, themed launches, and product/brand launches in Tanzania on a daily basis. With 11 years of experience in the marketing and branding industry, we specialize in providing our clients with tailored services at affordable prices.
